Revolutionizing learning through AI-powered question generation and feedback

Personalized questions and intelligent feedback are the keys to effective learning.

In today's educational landscape, students need more than just static content. They need dynamic, personalized learning experiences that challenge them and provide immediate, constructive feedback.

We've launched an innovative educational technology project with a clear mission: to develop an AI-powered system that generates tailored questions and provides intelligent feedback, ensuring students receive a truly personalized learning experience.

It's called Kora-Edu.

Our focus is twofold:

  1. AI Question Generation: Creating an AI system that can generate relevant, challenging questions across various subjects and difficulty levels, adapting to each student's learning pace and style.
  2. Intelligent Feedback: Developing an AI-powered feedback mechanism that not only assesses the correctness of answers but also provides detailed explanations, identifies knowledge gaps, and suggests personalized learning paths.

Our team and our entire product strategy are aligned to achieve these goals, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in AI-assisted education.

We approach education and artificial intelligence as intertwined domains, tackling technical challenges through innovative engineering and cutting-edge research. Our aim is to advance AI capabilities in natural language processing, question generation, and adaptive learning while maintaining a strong emphasis on pedagogical best practices and ethical considerations.

This way, we can enhance learning experiences and outcomes for students worldwide.

Our laser-focus means we're not distracted by unnecessary management overhead or diverse product lines. We're committed to developing a tool that genuinely revolutionizes how students learn and how educators teach.

We are a small, passionate team based in New Zealand, dedicated to transforming education through AI-powered question generation and feedback. Our project is open-source, and you can find our work on GitHub at https://github.com/kora-edu.
