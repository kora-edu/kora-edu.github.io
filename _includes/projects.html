Our Development Journey

Concept & Ideation

  • Research & Planning
  • Initial User Interviews

We began our journey by researching educational needs, exploring AI potential, and brainstorming features that could best support students and educators.

Prototype Development

  • Early Product Design
  • Feedback Collection

Our prototype was developed to test core features and gather feedback. This phase allowed us to refine functionality and design based on user feedback.

MVP Launch

  • Core Features Implemented
  • Initial Testing & Feedback

We launched our Minimum Viable Product (MVP) with essential features, including personalized study resources and AI-generated practice exams. This phase focuses on iterating based on real user interactions.

Scaling & Growth

  • Feature Expansion
  • Preparing for Public Launch

Our current focus is on expanding features, refining the user experience, and preparing for a full launch. This stage includes adding advanced insights and data-driven feedback for deeper learning support.

Future Vision

  • AI-Enhanced Tutoring
  • Collaboration with Educational Institutions

Looking ahead, we aim to integrate KORA with educational institutions and expand our AI capabilities for personalized tutoring. We envision a platform that serves both students and educators on a global scale.
